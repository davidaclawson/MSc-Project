Hybrid Cybersecurity Framework for Football Clubs

Key Information

The website created here has been developed as part of the research project delivered by David Lawson (1508178) as a requirement of completion towards the MSc Information Technology with Cyber Security course.


The rationale behind this research project was to create a Cybersecurity Framework consisting of the best aspects of already exisitng Cybersecurity Frameworks, and developing it with the idea of it being used by football clubs, and potentially other sporting organisations alike.


The hyperlinks located in the navigation panels in the header and footer will direct the user to key information involved in this research project.


The information presented here includes, but is not limited to, the following:

  • Case Study Information
  • Research Findings
  • Newly developed Hybrid Cybersecurity Framework
  • Key Sources and Tools


Please note, that all identifiable infomration of the case studies has been redacted to ensure adherance to an appropriate standard of ethics set forth by the British Computing Society.
